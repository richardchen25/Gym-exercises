# 🔴 Advanced Program (2+ years)

**Goal:** Continued growth through higher volume and effort management.
**Structure:** 6-day Push/Pull/Legs, run twice. Work sets at RPE 7–9 (1–3 reps in reserve). Rest 2–4 min on heavy compounds.

| Day | Session |
|---|---|
| Mon | Push A |
| Tue | Pull A |
| Wed | Legs A |
| Thu | Push B |
| Fri | Pull B |
| Sat | Legs B |
| Sun | Rest |

### Push A (strength bias)
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | RPE |
|---|---|---|
| Barbell Bench Press | 4 × 5–6 | 8 |
| Overhead Press | 3 × 6–8 | 8 |
| Incline Dumbbell Press | 3 × 8–10 | 8 |
| Cable Lateral Raise | 4 × 12–15 | 9 |
| Overhead Tricep Extension | 3 × 10–12 | 9 |

### Pull A (strength bias)
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | RPE |
|---|---|---|
| Weighted Pull-Up | 4 × 5–8 | 8 |
| Barbell Row | 4 × 6–8 | 8 |
| Chest-Supported Row | 3 × 10–12 | 8 |
| Rear Delt Fly | 3 × 15–20 | 9 |
| Barbell Curl | 3 × 8–10 | 9 |

### Legs A (squat bias)

## Managing fatigue

Deload every 4–6 weeks (same movements, ~60–70% load, two-thirds the sets). If two sessions in a row feel unusually heavy at normal weights, deload early. See [docs/progression-guide.md](../docs/progression-guide.md).
